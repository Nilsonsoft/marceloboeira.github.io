About me

Well, I'm a nerd since ... ever. I spent almost all my childhood fixing neighbors computers, blowing some old microwaves,
At that time I did not think of it as a profession, but the years have gone by and I just realize that I was made for this.

Sou um Nerd desde sempre, e passei a maioria da infância arrumando os computadores dos vizinhos, explodindo microondas velhos,
desmontanto televisões, etc. Nesta época eu não pensava nisto como uma profissão, mas os anos se passaram e eu acabei por entender que eu fui feito pra isso.
I dedicated myself to learn as much as possible about computers, how they worked. I understood that it needed to understand about electronics. One thing led to another and I ended up majoring in Industrial Electricity by SENAI.

Currently I dedicate myself to college of Electronics Engineering. But there are more than 5 years that I've been working with Software Development for private companies.
I am also a teacher, I love to share knowledge on the courses that I teach online.
If you like electronics, microcontroller programming ... be sure to visit my blog :)
